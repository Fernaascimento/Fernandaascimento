The Essence of Design

More than decoration, we create atmosphere

Founded in the vibrant heart of São Paulo by Fernanda Nascimento de Almeida, our firm represents the perfect intersection of artistic expression and architectural functionality. We believe that specialized interior design is not merely about aesthetics, but about deeply improving the quality of life through spatial harmony and flow.

With years of experience in professional design services, we navigate the complex balance between bold, modern trends and timeless, enduring comfort. Our team approaches every project as a collaborative journey, ensuring that the final result reflects the client's distinct personality while adhering to the highest technical standards.

From luxury private residences to dynamic corporate offices, Fernanda Design is committed to sustainable practices and meticulous attention to detail. We transform empty rooms into living, breathing experiences that inspire and comfort.

Curated Design Solutions

Comprehensive services for every scale

Residential Metamorphosis

We specialize in turning houses into sanctuaries by rigorously analyzing light, spatial flow, and personal taste. Our team curates furniture, color palettes, and textures that evoke deep comfort while maximizing the utility of every square meter. Whether it is a single-room refresh or a whole-home renovation, we manage the entire lifecycle of the decoration process.

Corporate Identity Interiors

We translate your brand's ethos into a physical environment that boosts employee productivity and leaves a lasting impression on clients. By optimizing spatial planning and integrating ergonomic furniture with bold branding elements, we create workspaces that are both visually stunning and highly functional. Our designs help businesses establish a strong physical presence.

Styling & Art Curation

Not every project requires construction; sometimes a space simply needs the right eye for styling, art selection, and lighting. We offer specialized consultation services to select art pieces, accessories, and textiles that elevate existing structures without major renovations. This service is perfect for clients seeking a quick yet high-impact aesthetic upgrade.
